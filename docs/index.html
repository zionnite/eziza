How it works

Three steps, no guesswork about where your package is.

No dispatch algorithm quietly assigning you whoever's closest. You see every offer before anything moves.

01

Post what needs moving

Drop a pin for pickup and drop-off, describe the package, and it goes live to riders and fleets nearby.

02

Compare offers, accept one

See each rider's or fleet's price, rating, and photo before you accept — pay straight from your Eziza wallet.

03

Track it, confirm with a code

Watch the live map from pickup to your door, then a one-time code — not just a status flag — proves it arrived.
